INTRERFACE FLOORING <IFSIA> FILES FOR OFFERING
Interface Flooring Systems Inc said it has filed for an offering of 2,800,000 Class A common shares through underwriters led by Robinson-Humphrey Co Inc and PaineWebber Group Inc <PWJ>.     The company said as previously announced, it will use proceeds to finance a tender offer to acquire the 50 pct of Debron Investments PLC it does not already own and to repay debt.  It said if it acquires all of Debron, it will repurchase 900,000 Class A shares now owned by Debron and hold them in its treasury.
