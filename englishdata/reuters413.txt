QUEBEC'S CAISSE DES JARDINS ISSUES YEN EUROBOND
Caisse Centrale Desjardins du Quebec is issuing a 10 billion yen step-up eurobond maturing on March 25, 1992 and priced at 101-3/4 pct, joint-bookrunner Warburg Securities said.     The issues pays a coupon of one pct in years one and two and then pays a coupon of 7-7/8 pct in years three, four and five. The selling concession is 1-1/4 pct while management and underwriting combined pays 5/8 pct.     The payment date is March 25 while the issue will be listed in Luxembourg. The borrower is the wholesale financing arm of a major group of credit unions in the Province of Quebec.
