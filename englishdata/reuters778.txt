STAR TECHNOLOGIES TO TRADE ON NASDAQ
Star Technologies Inc said its shares will begin trading on the Nasdaq national market system tomorrow.     The company, a manufacturer of very high-speed computers for scientific applications, has annual revenues of about 40 mln dlrs.     In the nine months ended Dec 31, the company earned 1.9 mln dlrs against a loss of 16.5 mln dlrs in the year-ago period, as sales rose to 32.6 mln dlrs from 9.3 mln dlrs.
