COUSINS HOME FURNISHINGS <CUZZ> GET CREDIT LINE
Cousins Home Furnishings Inc said it obtained a 5.0-mln-dlr working capital line of credit from Lloyds Bank PLC, through its Los Angeles branch.     The credit is supported by a guarantee from the company's Canadian affiliate, The Brick Warehouse Ltd, which will receive 200,000-dlrs worth of the company's stock.     The line of credit, which expires in July, 1989, will be used to pay off a 1,750,000-dlr working capital loan the company owes Wells Farg Bank.
