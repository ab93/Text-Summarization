AVX CORP <AVX> DEBT AFFIRMED BY S/P
Standard and Poor's Corp said it affirmed the B-plus rating on AVX Corp's 28 mln dlrs of subordinated debt.     The company's implied senior debt rating is BB.     S and P said AVX's purchase offer for CTS Corp <CTS> has expired and the acquisition does not appear likely to occur. AVX was listed on S and P's creditwatch with negative implications on December 17, 1986, because of the proposed acquisition.
