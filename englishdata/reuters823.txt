AMERICAN ELECTRIC <AEP> UNIT REDEEMING BONDS
Appalachian Power Co, a unit of American Electric Power Co, said it will redeem on May one an additional 1.38 mln dlrs of its 12-7/8 pct first mortgage bonds due 2013.     That increases to 2.88 mln dlrs the amount of bonds the utility will buy back. Appalachian Power said it will redeem the bonds at par plus accrued interest.
