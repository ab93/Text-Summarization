EC, EURATOM FRENCH FRANC EUROBONDS EXPECTED
The European Community (EC) and Euratom, the European atomic energy agency, are expected to issue French franc eurobonds this month, a Treasury spokesman said.     Other eurofranc bonds this month are likely to include issues by a bank and a company, both unidentified, but no other details were available. The spokesman said the March calendar would be flexible, to take account of market conditions.
