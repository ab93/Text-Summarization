FOOTHILL <GFI> ARRANGES DEBT PLACEMENT
Foothill Group Inc said its Foothill Capital Corp unit arranged the private placement of 23 mln dlrs in senior debt and 27 mln in senior subordinated debt.     The senior and senior subordinated debt was purchased by institutional lenders and will bear interest at 9.4 pct and 10.15 pct, respectively, Foothill said.     It said completion of the transaction will increase the company's capital funds to 138 mln dlrs.
