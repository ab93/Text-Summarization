PALL CORP <PLL> SECOND QUARTER SALES, ORDERS UP
Pall Corp said sales for the second quarter ended January 31 were up 17.5 pct to 94.0 mln dlrs from 80.0 mln dlrs a year before and orders were up 15.5 pct to 101.1 mln from 87.4 mln.     For the first half, it said sales were up 16.0 pct to 174.6 mln dlrs from 150.3 mln dlrs a year before, with orders up* 13.0 pct to 189.6 mln from 167.7 mln.
