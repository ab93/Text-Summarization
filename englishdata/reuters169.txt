(CORRECTED) - BANPONCE <BDEP> PLACES NOTES
BanPonce Corp said it privately placed 30 mln dlrs of its 8.25 pct senior notes due 1992 through Lincoln National Investment Management Co, New York Life Insurance Co and Dillon Read and Co Inc.     It said proceeds will be used to increase working capital, for general corporate purposes, and for possible future acquisitions.     - Corrects dollar figure of notes placed in item appearing Feb 25.
