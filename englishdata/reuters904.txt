NEDERLANDSE GASUNIE ISSUES 100 MLN DLR EUROBOND
NV Nederlandse Gasunie is issuing a 100 mln dlr eurobond due April 15, 1992 paying 7-1/4 pct and priced at 101-1/8 pct, lead manager Citicorp Investment Bank Ltd said.     The non-callable bond is available in denominations of 5,000 dlrs and will be listed in Luxembourg. The selling concession is 1-1/4 pct, while management and underwriting combined pays 5/8 pct.     The payment date is April 15.
