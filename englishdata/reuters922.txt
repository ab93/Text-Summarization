MONTEDISON UNIT ISSUES 50 MLN AUSTRALIAN DLR BOND
Montedison Finance Overseas Ltd, a unit of Montedison SpA, is issuing a 50 mln Australian dlr eurobond due April 3, 1990 paying 15-1/2 pct and priced at 101-3/8 pct, lead manager Orion Royal Bank Ltd said.     The non-callable bond is guaranteed by the parent. The selling concession is one pct while management and underwriting combined pays 1/2 pct.     The issue will be listed in Luxembourg and is available in denominations of 1,000 and 10,000 Australian dlrs. The payment date is April 3.
