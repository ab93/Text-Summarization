SCOTTY'S <SHB> SALES UP FIVE PCT IN FEBRUARY
Scotty's Inc said sales for the four weeks ended February 21 rose five pct, to 41.7 mln dlrs, from 39.8 mln dlrs for the corresponding period last year.     Scotty's said sales for the 34 weeks ended February 21 totaled 323.2 mln dlrs, up six pct over sales of 305.7 mln dlrs for the same period in 1986.     The company, which operates 137 stores, said sales were up in both the consumer "do-it-yourself" and professional builders markets.
