CCX NETWORK <CCXN> SECONDARY OFFERING UNDERWAY
CCX Network Inc said it was offering 220,838 shares of stock at 15.75 dlrs a share though underwriters Stephens Inc and Cazenove Inc.     The company said it was selling the stock on behalf of some shareholders, including those who recently received shares in the company in exchange for their businesses.     The company said  it was receiving no proceeds from the offering.
