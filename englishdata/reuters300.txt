MERRILL LYNCH ISSUES 100 MLN ECU EUROBOND
Merrill Lynch and Co is issuing a 100 mln Ecu eurobond due March 30, 1990, paying 7-3/8 pct and priced at 101 pct, lead manager Banque Paribas Capital Markets said.     Merrill Lynch Capital Markets is co-lead manager.     The issue is available in denominations of 1,000 and 10,00 Ecus and will be listed in Luxembourg. The payment date is March 30, 1990.     The selling concession is 1-3/8 pct while management and underwriting combined pays 1/2 pct.
